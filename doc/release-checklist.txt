Checklist for tasks related to release of a new version
=======================================================

- change version in build/manifest.properties and examples/web/inc/page_header.jsp
- commit everything
- cvs tag in format "rel_1_0_3"
- run ant "all"
- upload files to SF
- add new release at SF
- post news at SF
- post news article in wiki
- post announcement on Sun JSF forum
- announce on both mailing lists
